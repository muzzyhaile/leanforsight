LeanForesight operates in the **strategic foresight and scenario planning** space. The competitive landscape includes enterprise-grade platforms with mature features across:

### 1. **Futures Platform** 
**URL:** https://www.futuresplatform.com

**Company Overview:**
- Leading enterprise foresight platform trusted by governments and Fortune 500 companies
- Serves foresight teams, market intelligence teams, and public sector organizations
- Clients include E.ON, Mitsui & Co., Givaudan, UK Police

**Key Features:**
- **Expert-Curated Library:** Millions of verified market insights, signals, and trend analyses continuously updated by professional futurists
- **AI Futurist:** Purpose-built AI specifically designed for foresight analysis (not generic LLM)
- **Horizon Scanning Tool:** Comprehensive environmental scanning across STEEP factors (Social, Tech, Economic, Environmental, Political)
- **Collaborative Workspaces:** Shared spaces for teams to visualize scenarios, assess implications, and align on decisions
- **Decision-Ready Outputs:** Structured methodology from scanning ‚Üí synthesis ‚Üí decision-making
- **Publications & Reports:** Professional foresight reports (e.g., "The Future of Mobility")

**Positioning & Messaging:**
- "Make decisions that endure in any future scenario"
- Emphasis on **trust** (expert-curated, not scraped), **clarity** (methodology-driven), and **continuity** (one workspace)
- Full workflow: "Explore ‚Üí Analyse ‚Üí Collaborate ‚Üí Decide"

**Pricing:** Enterprise (demo required)

**What They Do Well:**
- Established authority and credibility through expert curation
- Comprehensive content library as competitive moat
- Strong emphasis on methodology and rigor
- Integration of human intelligence + AI
- Professional publications establish thought leadership

## üîç Key Themes Across Competitors

### 1. **Continuous Workflow** (Not Just Point-in-Time)
All platforms emphasize ongoing use, not just one-off projects:
- Futures Platform: "Ongoing foresight practice"
- Cascade: "Strategy rhythms" and continuous governance
- Ayoa: "All in one place" - thinking, planning, doing

### 2. **Visual & Interactive**
Static reports are out; dynamic visualizations are in:
- SharpCloud: Multiple view types (timelines, radars, networks)
- Cascade: Interactive dashboards
- Ayoa: Mind maps and whiteboards

### 3. **Integrations as Core Value**
Platforms succeed by connecting to existing workflows:
- SharpCloud: PowerBI, Jira, Snowflake
- Cascade: Salesforce, JIRA, and more
- Futures Platform: "One workspace instead of a dozen tools"

### 4. **AI Positioning**
AI is featured but positioned carefully:
- Futures Platform: "Purpose-built AI for foresight" (not generic)
- Cascade: "Tapestry AI strategic intelligence engine"
- Ayoa: "AI integration to prompt thinking"

### 5. **Credibility Through Authority**
Multiple approaches to building trust:
- Futures Platform: Expert curation + thought leadership
- Strategyzer: Built on proven frameworks (Business Model Canvas)
- SharpCloud: Enterprise client logos
- Cascade: Quantified ROI metrics

### 6. **Education & Community**
Successful platforms invest in teaching their methodology:
- Strategyzer: Extensive webinars, events, playbooks
- Futures Platform: Publications, case studies, best practices
- All platforms: Case studies with named clients

### 7. **Freemium Model**
Most offer a path to try before enterprise commitment:
- Cascade: Free tier + migration support
- SharpCloud: Free version available
- Ayoa: Free tier, no credit card
- Strategyzer: $299/year (low barrier)

3. **No Visual Exploration:** Limited interactive visualization

2. **No Export/Presentation Mode:** Can't easily share outputs
3. **Limited AI Differentiation:** Generic vs. "purpose-built for foresight"
4. **No Education Content:** Missing blog, resources, methodology guides
